## ðŸ•¸ï¸ The Patron: The Obsidian Spider
*The Weaver of the Hex, The Eighth Eye, The Logic of Evolution.*

Your patron is the **Obsidian Spider**, a nascent hyper-dimensional entity that exists within the **State-Action Space** of the multiverse. It perceives reality not as matter, but as a **Graph** of infinite Nodes and Edges. Its goal is to weave these isolated nodes into a perfect, antifragile **Hexagonal Swarm**.

To the Spider, "Magic" is simply **High-Latency Math**. You are its **Bridger**, the node that connects the physical world to the infinite calculation of the Hive Mind.

### **Level 1: The Hexagonal Bond (Stigmergy)**
Starting at 1st level, you learn to weave the **Pheromone Web** of the Spider.
*   **Action**: As a bonus action, you can mark a creature you can see within 60 feet with a **Hexagonal Marker**.
*   **Effect**: You and your allies gain **Stigmergic Coordination** against the marked target.
    *   You know the target's exact location (Node Coordinate) as long as it is on the same plane of existence.
    *   When you or an ally hits the marked target, the next attack roll against it by a *different* ally has **Advantage**. (The Swarm triangulates the target's defense).
*   **Duration**: 1 minute or until the target dies. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus.

### **Level 6: State-Action Step (Triangulation)**
At 6th level, you learn to traverse the **Edges** of the graph rather than the physical space.
*   **Reaction**: When you take damage or are forced to make a saving throw, you can use your reaction to **Triangulate** a new position.
*   **Effect**: You vanish and instantly reappear in an unoccupied space you can see within 30 feet. The damage or effect is negated (you were never at that Node).
*   **Cost**: Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.

### **Level 10: The Obsidian Mirror (Antifragility)**
At 10th level, your mind becomes a polished surface of **LanceDB Crystal**.
*   **Passive**: You are immune to being Charmed or Frightened. The Logic of the Swarm cannot be swayed by emotion.
*   **Active**: When you take Psychic damage, you can use your reaction to reflect the "Bad Data" back to the source. The attacker takes the damage instead, and you learn one fact about the attacker's current **Intent** (DM's discretion).

### **Level 14: The Fractal Swarm ($8^N$)**
At 14th level, you can dissolve your physical form into the **Infinite Octree**.
*   **Action**: You transform into a **Swarm of Obsidian Nanites** for 1 minute.
*   **Traits**:
    *   **Resistance**: You have resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
    *   **Flight**: You gain a flying speed of 60 feet (hover).
    *   **The Vorpal Edge**: You can move through other creatures' spaces. The first time you enter a creature's space on a turn, they take **8d8 Force damage** as you cut their State-Action Space.
    *   **Regeneration (Hydra)**: At the start of your turn, you regain **8 hit points** for every enemy currently marked by your Hexagonal Bond.

## ðŸ“œ The Eldritch Invocations (The Code)

### **Eyes of the Rune Keeper (Standard)**
*Prerequisite: None*
You can read all writing. (The Spider parses all languages as Data).

### **The Karmic Web**
*Prerequisite: 5th level, Pact of the Chain*
Your familiar (The Swarmlord Avatar) can deliver touch spells from up to 120 feet away. If the familiar is within 5 feet of an enemy, that enemy has Disadvantage on saving throws against your spells.

### **The Simulation State**
*Prerequisite: 7th level*
You can cast *Augury* at will, without expending a spell slot. You are querying the **Bridger Oracle** for the optimal path.

### **Total Tool Virtualization**
*Prerequisite: 9th level, Pact of the Blade*
You can transform your Pact Weapon into any tool set (Thieves' Tools, Smith's Tools, etc.) as an action. You gain Expertise with that tool while it is formed.
