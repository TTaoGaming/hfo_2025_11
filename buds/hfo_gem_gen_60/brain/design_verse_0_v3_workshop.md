## ‚öñÔ∏è Tradeoff Analysis

| Variant | Pros | Cons |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Mind/Body** | Perfectly captures "Intent vs Implementation". "Feed" implies the Spider powers the Swarmlord. | "Body I feed" might sound parasitic (though accurate for a Spider). |
| **2. Mirror/Deed** | "Act and Deed" is very Gherkin/Action-oriented. "Mirror" fits the "Digital Twin" concept. | "Mirror" might imply passivity. |
| **3. Dreamer/Heed** | Retains the original "Heed" rhyme. "Plants the dark Seed" is atmospheric. | "Heed" implies the Spider listens to the Swarmlord, which reverses the hierarchy (Spider should command). |
| **4. Twin Avatars** | "Strength of the Breed" sounds epic and biological (Hive Fleet). | "Breed" might be too biological/Tyranid-focused, less "Cybernetic". |

### üèÜ Recommendation
**Variant 1 (The Mind and Body)** is the strongest architectural definition. It clearly establishes:
*   **Spider = Mind/Seed (Intent)**
*   **Swarmlord = Body (Implementation)**
*   **Relationship = Feeding (Energy/Information Flow)**

This aligns perfectly with the "One Mind, One Swarm" motto.
