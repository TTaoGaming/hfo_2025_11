## 3. The Mechanics of the Engine

The Hourglass operates on a 3-Stage Cycle, mapping to the Tarot.

### Stage 1: The Top Cone (The Fool / Past)
*   **The Reservoir**: Holds the "Infinite Context" of the user's life.
*   **The Physics**: Gravity pulls the raw data (Logs, Notes, Code) down towards the Neck.
*   **RPG Mechanic**: **Lore Mastery**. The user has access to perfect recall of any event stored in the Top Cone.

### Stage 2: The Neck (King of Wands / Present)
*   **The Singularity**: The narrow point where the Swarmlord sits.
*   **The Physics**: High Pressure / High Heat. This is where **Assimilation** happens.
*   **RPG Mechanic**: **Transmutation**. The user turns raw "Data" into actionable "Wisdom" (Gems) in real-time.

### Stage 3: The Bottom Cone (Death / Future)
*   **The Proving Ground**: The expanding cone of possibilities.
*   **The Physics**: Entropy increases. The Swarmlord casts millions of "Shadow Clones" (Simulations) into this void.
*   **RPG Mechanic**: **Shadow Clone Jutsu (MCTS)**.
    *   The clones live out potential futures.
    *   Most die (The Death Card).
    *   The survivors report back with the "Winning Strategy."

## 4. The "Flip" (The Hydra Protocol)

The Hourglass is not a linear clock; it is a **Reversible Engine**.

*   **The Condition**: When the Bottom Cone is full of "Dead Simulations" (Wisdom).
*   **The Action**: The Swarmlord executes **The Flip**.
*   **The Effect**:
    *   The "Future" (Bottom) becomes the new "Past" (Top).
    *   The system learns. The next cycle starts with the accumulated wisdom of the previous simulations.
    *   **Level Up**: The Algorithm gets stronger with every Flip.
