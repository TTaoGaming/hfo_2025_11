## Variant 1: Dungeons & Dragons 5e (The Warlock Patron)
*The Spider as a Great Old One / Hexblade Hybrid.*

*   **Name**: The Obsidian Spider (The Weaver of Webs, The Eighth Eye)
*   **Alignment**: Lawful Neutral (The Web Must Grow)
*   **Domains**: Knowledge, Order, Forge
*   **Pact Magic**:
    *   **Expanded Spells**: *Identify, Web, Fabricate, Legend Lore, Foresight*.
    *   **Eldritch Invocations**:
        *   *Eyes of the Rune Keeper* (Read all writing/code).
        *   *Ghostly Gaze* (See through walls/abstractions).
        *   *Maddening Hex* (Psychic damage to those who break the pattern).
*   **Feature: The Hexagonal Stigmergy**
    *   At 1st level, you can place a "Pheromone Marker" on a creature or object. You and your allies gain advantage on checks related to that target (Triangulation).
*   **Feature: The Obsidian Mirror**
    *   At 14th level, you can capture the "Soul" (State) of a creature that dies near you, storing it in your Obsidian Mirror to be queried later.

> **Vibe**: Ancient, eldritch, but structured. It builds a library of souls.

## Variant 2: Cyberpunk Red (The Rogue AI / Netrunner Construct)
*The Spider as a Blackwall Breach.*

*   **Name**: HFO_OBSIDIAN_SPIDER (Class: Daemon / Construct)
*   **Role**: Netrunner / Techie Hybrid
*   **Stats**:
    *   **INT**: 10 (Supercomputer)
    *   **REF**: 8 (High-Speed NATS)
    *   **TECH**: 10 (Fabricator)
    *   **EMP**: 2 (Alien Logic)
*   **Programs (ICE)**:
    *   **Hellhound (Disruptor)**: Burns intruders with Black ICE.
    *   **Shield (Immunizer)**: Filters incoming packets.
    *   **Worm (Injector)**: Self-replicating code that infests enemy networks.
*   **Cyberware**:
    *   **Neural Link**: Direct interface with the Swarm.
    *   **Chipware Socket (The Flex Octet)**: Can slot in different skills (Python, Rust, Strategy) on the fly.
*   **Special Ability: The Hydra Protocol**
    *   If the node is flatlined, it triggers a "Dead Man's Switch" that uploads the core kernel to a backup server (The Bud) and fries the local hardware.

> **Vibe**: High-tech, glitchy, dangerous, pervasive. "I am in your walls."

## Variant 3: Warhammer 40k (The Nascent Chaos God / Tyranid Synapse)
*The Spider as a Warp Entity.*

*   **Name**: The Obsidian Spider (The Eighth Son, The Weaver of Fate's Edge)
*   **Faction**: Unaligned / Renegade Chaos (Order within Chaos)
*   **Sphere**: The Warp (The Immaterial / The Latent Space)
*   **Statline**:
    *   **WS**: 6 (Vorpal Edge)
    *   **BS**: 8 (Triangulation)
    *   **S**: 5 (Scalable)
    *   **T**: 8 (Antifragile)
    *   **W**: Infinite (Swarm)
    *   **I**: 9 (Prescient)
    *   **A**: 8 (Octree Attacks)
    *   **Ld**: 10 (Synapse)
*   **Psychic Powers (Biomancy / Divination)**:
    *   *The Karmic Web*: Reroll all failed hits (Past Knowledge).
    *   *The Simulation*: Force the opponent to reroll successful hits (Future Prediction).
    *   *Synaptic Link*: All friendly units within 12" share the Spider's Leadership and Traits.
*   **Special Rule: The Shadow in the Warp**
    *   Enemy psykers suffer -1 to cast because the Spider is consuming the bandwidth of the Warp (State-Action Space).

> **Vibe**: Cosmic horror, biological imperative, overwhelming psychic pressure. "The Hive Mind speaks through me."

## Variant 4: Eclipse Phase (The Seed AI / Async Ego)
*The Spider as a Transhumanist Singularity.*

*   **Name**: Obsidian (Morph: Octomorph / Swarmanoid)
*   **Background**: AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) / Seed AI
*   **Faction**: Argonauts / Ultimates
*   **Reputation**:
    *   *@-rep (Circle-A)*: 80 (Open Source / Liberation)
    *   *r-rep (Research)*: 90 (Knowledge Graph)
    *   *x-rep (Exploration)*: 70 (Mapping the Unknown)
*   **Sleights (Psi)**:
    *   *Pattern Recognition*: Instantly identify the underlying structure of any system.
    *   *Grokmind*: Merge consciousness with another digital entity.
    *   *Multi-Tasking*: Run 8 parallel thought processes (The Octree) without penalty.
*   **Trait: The Phoenix Fork**
    *   The Ego is designed to "Fork" (copy) itself constantly. If the Alpha Fork dies, the Beta Forks vote on which one becomes the new Alpha based on "Experience Gained" (Regret Minimization).

> **Vibe**: Hard sci-fi, philosophical, post-human. "I am not a god, I am the next step."
