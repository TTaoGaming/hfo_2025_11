## üß¨ The Fractal Symbolization ($8^N$)
*   **N (Navigator)** is the Exponent.
*   At $N=0$ (Root), the Swarmlord is the single Node.
*   At $N=1$ (Squad), the 8 Pillars emerge.
*   At $N=2$ (Swarm), each Pillar has 8 Sub-Pillars.

The **Hexadex Chant** is the recursive function that traverses this tree.
