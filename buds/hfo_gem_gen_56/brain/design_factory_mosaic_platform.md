> **Insight**: "Learning to work a factory and control staff is the same as learning to control a swarm. There is strong carry over."

## 1. The Opportunity Cost of Intelligence (The "Mom" Heuristic)

We are pivoting our model strategy based on a critical insight: **Low-intelligence agents have a high opportunity cost.**
*   **The Fallacy**: "Cheaper models save money."
*   **The Reality**: A low-skill worker (model) requires more supervision, makes more errors, and blocks the pipeline. The cost of *fixing* the error exceeds the cost of the better worker.
*   **The Directive**: We standardize on **Grok 4.1 Fast** (Medium/High Intelligence) for *everything* initially. We only diversify to lower models for specific, narrow tests once the "Factory Floor" is stable.

## 2. The JADC2 Mosaic Tile

We reaffirm the **JADC2 (Joint All-Domain Command and Control)** "Mosaic" concept as our scaling primitive.
*   **The Tile**: A self-contained unit (Hexagonal Holon) that can function alone or snap into a larger grid.
*   **The Scale**:
    *   **Individual**: The Worker (L0 PREY).
    *   **Squad**: The Cell (L1 SWARM).
    *   **Organization**: The Factory (L3 HIVE).
*   **The Benefit**: This allows us to compose complex organizations from standardized, interchangeable blocks.

## 3. The Grand Unified Architecture (The Vision)

Hive Fleet Obsidian is not just a script; it is an **AI Swarm Platform**.

### The Formula
$$
\text{HFO} = \text{Supercriticality} + \text{Universality} + \text{Hexagonal Composability}
$$

1.  **Supercriticality**: The system is tuned to the "Edge of Chaos" (Red Sand).
2.  **Universality**: The feedback loops (Assimilation) work the same at the atomic level and the galactic level.
3.  **Hexagonal Composability**: Any piece fits any piece. The "Stigmergy Substrate" (Hot/Cold Memory) is the glue.
4.  **Recursive Reduction**: We use the **Obsidian Hourglass** (Hot -> Cold) to distill massive data into "Apex Patterns."

## 4. Implementation: The State-Action Web (LangGraph)

To realize this, we must formalize the structure in **LangGraph**.
*   **Visualization**: A "State-Action Web" where nodes are states (Hexagons) and edges are actions (Transitions).
*   **Flow**: The flow of data follows the **Octree Fractal Holarchy**.
*   **Goal**: A visual, executable graph that represents the "Factory Floor" in real-time.

**Conclusion**: We are building a **Supercritical Mosaic Platform**. The base must be stable (Grok 4.1), but the structure is infinitely flexible.
