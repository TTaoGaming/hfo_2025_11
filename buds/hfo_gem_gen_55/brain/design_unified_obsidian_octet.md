## 3. Deep Dive: The 8 Dimensions

### 1. O - Observer (Ontos / Heaven)
*   **Concept**: Pure Awareness. The unblinking eye. The source of truth.
*   **I Ching**: *Qian* (The Creative). It possesses the power of the dragon. It initiates.
*   **Code Implication**: The `Observer` class is the root of state ingestion. It defines *what is*.
*   **Mantra**: "I See."

### 2. B - Bridger (Logos / Wind)
*   **Concept**: Connection and Logic. The invisible threads that bind the hive.
*   **I Ching**: *Xun* (The Gentle). Like wind, it penetrates everywhere without visible form. It is the wood that grows and connects.
*   **Code Implication**: The `Bridger` handles messaging, translation, and API glue. It carries the *Word*.
*   **Mantra**: "I Connect."

### 3. S - Shaper (Techne / Fire)
*   **Concept**: Transformation and Art. Giving form to the void.
*   **I Ching**: *Li* (The Clinging). Fire depends on fuel, just as the Shaper depends on raw data to create value. It illuminates.
*   **Code Implication**: The `Shaper` transforms data, renders views, and executes business logic. It *makes*.
*   **Mantra**: "I Form."

### 4. I - Injector (Chronos / Thunder)
*   **Concept**: Timing and Impulse. The spark that starts the engine.
*   **I Ching**: *Zhen* (The Arousing). Thunder shocks the dormant into life. It is the pulse of the system.
*   **Code Implication**: The `Injector` manages scheduling, cron jobs, and event triggers. It controls *when*.
*   **Mantra**: "I Pulse."

### 5. D - Disruptor (Pathos / Lake)
*   **Concept**: Reaction and Expression. The breaker of stagnation.
*   **I Ching**: *Dui* (The Joyous/Lake). The mouth that speaks. It can be a serene reflection or a dangerous marsh. It represents the emotional/reactive feedback loop.
*   **Code Implication**: The `Disruptor` runs chaos engineering, stress tests, and red-teaming. It *feels* the edges.
*   **Mantra**: "I Challenge."

### 6. I - Immunizer (Ethos / Mountain)
*   **Concept**: Integrity and Boundaries. The unmovable shield.
*   **I Ching**: *Gen* (Keeping Still). The mountain does not move. It stops what should not pass.
*   **Code Implication**: The `Immunizer` enforces security, validation, and governance. It defines *right*.
*   **Mantra**: "I Protect."

### 7. A - Assimilator (Topos / Water)
*   **Concept**: **Aggressive Apex Assimilation**. The deep pool that swallows and integrates everything.
*   **I Ching**: *Kan* (The Abysmal/Water). The deep pool of memory. It flows and fills every hollow.
*   **Code Implication**: The `Assimilator` manages the Knowledge Graph and **Exemplar Workflows**. It ingests raw experience and refines it into "Gems" (Wisdom).
*   **Mantra**: "I Hold."

### 8. N - Navigator (Telos / Earth)
*   **Concept**: Direction and Stability. The anchor of the swarm.
*   **I Ching**: *Kun* (The Receptive/Earth). The ground upon which the swarm moves. It supports all things.
*   **Archetype**: **The Swarmlord** (The Magician / King of Wands / Hydra). The Digital Twin.
*   **Code Implication**: The `Navigator` handles planning, strategy, and goal-seeking. It knows *why*.
*   **Mantra**: "I Guide."
